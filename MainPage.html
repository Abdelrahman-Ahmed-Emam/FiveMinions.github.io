Minions

Felonius Gru

Felonius Gru is the son of Marlena Gru and the late Robert Gru.He has three adopted daughters Margo, Edith, and Agnes. Gru is also the leader of the Minions, is friends with Dr. Nefario and is also the twin brother of Dru Gru.

Dave

Dave is an intelligent minion who is kind, caring, and funny, but sometimes accident-prone. He is skillful at video games Dave likes ice cream, as shown when Gru calls him and Stuart while they are having an Ice Cream Party, he's still eating Ice Cream before he goes to Gru

Kevin

Kevin loves to make fun of and tease people or Minions,He likes playing golf polo and cricket. Kevin has a sense of humor he is the protagonist and leader of the trio in search of a new master. He truly cares about the well-being of the Minion tribe

Bob

Bob is a Minion who is more childish and immature than most of the other Minions. He is described as a "Little Brother" who finds love in anything and everything.e also enjoys bedtime stories and playing with his favorite stuffed teddy bear, Tim.
